Imp Notes-
* In case of binary, there are zeros to left of number
  (...000001010) but not to right because zeros to left
  has no meaning while adding zeros to right change number.

* So, in left shift, we easily move digits to left and fill
  empty spaces with zeros; like:
  10 << 2 => 1010 = 101000

* But in right shift, we can move digits to right but that
  digit ignores as there are no empty spaces on right side
  to hold that digit; like 10 >> 1 => 1010 = 101
